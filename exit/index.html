    LEAVING IT BETTER

    Take a moment to look around before you go.
    It takes only a sec, yet it matters more than you know.
    For the one who comes next or for you when you're back,
    a little effort now keeps everything on track.

    IMPORTANT

    • Turn off all HVAC units.
    • Unplug all television power cords from electrical outlets.
    • Close and lock all windows, positioning window blinds halfway.
    • Turn off all the lights.

    AS NEEDED

    • Unplug all items that were plugged in during the stay.
    • Wipe down the kitchen and all bathrooms using disinfecting wipes (located under the kitchen sink; extra in pantry).
    • Empty the kitchen and all bathroom trash cans, replacing the trash bags found at the bottom of each trash can.
    • Close all bathtub plugs and lift each toilet lid and seat.
    • If you used our towels or sheets, please place them in the laundry hamper located in the main bedroom closet; same-color replacement sheets for remaking the beds are located in the hallway closet near the main bedroom.

    LASTLY

    • Leave the keys in the drawer of the table next to the front door.
    • The front door will lock automatically when closed.
